How to play

First time on Dominet?
Dominion is a deckbuilding game where you build your deck as you play.
Turn:
Players start each turn with 1 action (to play action cards) and 1 buy (to buy new cards)
  1. Action Phase - Play action cards from your hand
  2. Treasure Phase - Play any treasures from your hand to gain $
  3. Buy Phase - buy cards from the supply to add to your deck
  4. Cleanup Phase - discard all cards played and in hand, draw 5 new cards
Terminology:
  • Trash - to remove from player's deck/discard completely (and placed into trash pile)
  • Balance - current $ in play
  • VP - Victory Points (Note: cards with VP are worth that VP just by being in the deck)
  • VP Token - Victory Point Token that is permanently added to your score area
  • Reshuffle - A player reshuffles when they run out of cards to draw, placing cards from their discard pile into their deck and shuffling
End Conditions:
The game ends when either
  • All Provinces or Colonies run out of supply
  • Any 3 cards run out of supply
The player with the most VP at the end of the game is victorious!
