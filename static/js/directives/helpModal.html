How to play

Dominion is a deckbuilding game where you build your deck as you play.
Turn:
Players start each turn with 1 action (to play action cards) and 1 buy (to buy new cards)
  1. Action Phase - Play action cards from your hand
  2. Treasure Phase - Play any treasures from your hand to gain $
  3. Buy Phase - buy cards from the supply to add to your deck
  4. Cleanup Phase - discard all cards played and in hand, draw 5 new cards
End Conditions:
The game ends when either
  1. All Provinces or Colonies run out of supply
  2. Any 3 cards run out of supply
The player with the most VP (Victory Points) at the end of the game is victorious!
